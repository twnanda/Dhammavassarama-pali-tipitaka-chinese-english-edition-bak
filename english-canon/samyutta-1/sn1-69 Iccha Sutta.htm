Samyutta Nikaya I.69

Iccha Sutta

Desire

Translated from the Pali by Thanissaro Bhikkhu.
For free distribution only.
[A deva:]
With what         is the world tied down?

    With the subduing
of what         is it freed?
    With the abandoning
of what         are all bonds
            cut through?

[The Buddha:]

With desire     the world is tied down.
    With the subduing
of desire         it's freed.

    With the abandoning
of desire         all bonds
            are cut through.
